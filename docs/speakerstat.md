## Notes on methodology

- Speakers are extracted from bullet lines in the per-year `YYYY.md` agenda files. Each bullet typically follows the pattern `- Talk Title, Speaker Name ([links])`.
- Multi-speaker sessions are split on `,`, ` and `, and ` & `. Filler words (`with`, `by`, `moderated by`) are stripped.
- A speaker is counted once per year regardless of how many sessions they delivered that year. The **# Sessions** column shows the total session count across all years.
- Manually merged aliases: `Nico Josuttis` ↔ `Nicolai Josuttis`, `Robert C. Seacord` ↔ `Robert Seacord`, `Hans Elias B. Josephsen` ↔ `Hans Elias Josephsen`, `Anastasia Kazakova` ↔ `Anastasiia Kazakova`, `Ole André V. Ravnås` ↔ `Ole André Vadla Ravnås`.
- A handful of non-talk bullets (e.g. NDC Party entries) and a few irregularly-formatted lines are not counted; these do not affect the ranking of the most prolific speakers.
